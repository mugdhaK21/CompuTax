About Us


We COMPU-TAX FORMS PVT. LTD located at Survey No. 75/76, Aher Estate, Walhekarwadi, Chinchwadgaon, Pune- 411033 glad to introduce ourselves as leading organization for the last 20 years in the field of Printing Business.

Our quality service and adequet experience in this field kept us ahead from others. We are capable to undertake all types of complex pre-press and printing jobs. The Printing Industry has grow in leaps and bounds over the past few years. Today, India is a major player in this industry and has a substantial market of all the printing done in the world.

When Compu-Tax was born, it was all very different back then. It is Often said that 'Great Happenings always have humble beginnings' and the same is true of all Great Legends. The birth of what is now Compu-Tax Forms Pvt. Ltd. dates back 20 years in the past.

With computerisation being in its early years then, Compu-Tax Forms preserved with the strong belief that No Legend ever expects an easy path to being the ideal that everybody looks up to. With our firm belief in quality and customer-satisfaction as our aim, Compu-Tax forms started on an uphill journey spanning two decades and there has never been a looking back since then.

Today with more than 500 clients bearing testimony to our claims, we great pride in mentioning that Compu-Tax Forms Pvt. Ltd. is today The Leader in Multi-Colour Offset Printing, the manufacturing of Pre-Printed and Plain/EZR Multi-Color Computer Stationery, Commercial Printing, Offset Printing, Labels, Barcoding Labels, Screen Printing and Binding.
